About the show and host

ABOUT THE SHOW

You Are a Lawyer is a podcast that shares the experiences and successes of law school graduates who created their own career paths. The podcast focuses on two distinct paths: those who, by choice or chance, have not been admitted to a bar association, yet use their legal education to create incredible careers, and those who are admitted to a state’s bar, but use their law degrees in innovative ways to create nontraditional, yet rewarding paths to success.

According to LEXICO, a lawyer is, “a person who practices or studies law.”  As a law school graduate, you are a lawyer, and through this podcast, we will share the challenges and triumphs of being a lawyer.

Whether you are a law student, chose a traditional career trajectory then left the practice of law, or stumbled into a unique career, this podcast is for you because You Are a Lawyer.

Note: The purpose of this podcast is to encourage ingenuity if your life after law school looks differently than you imagined. This show highlights the career paths of each guest and in no way discourages admittance to a bar association.

ABOUT THE HOST
LAWYER: A PERSON WHO PRACTICES OR STUDIES LAW

Kyla (Hemphill) Denanyoh is a 2015 graduate of Southern University Law Center. Kyla took the Louisiana bar exam, but did not pass.

Kyla was not the first person to not pass a bar exam, but the experience felt isolating. Her licensed friends ignored the subject altogether. The lawyers that she knew who also had not passed the bar exam, dealt with their pain in whispers.

After months of frustration and self-doubt, Kyla began to appreciate the power of her legal education and acknowledges that she could find career success in a non-traditional way.

Kyla is currently the Operations Manager of an intellectual property legal department in a mid-sized law firm; a role that was created for her unique abilities.

Kyla began to seek out other lawyers who were using their legal educations to create their own career paths and were willing to share their stories. The “You Are A Lawyer” podcast was created to take these whispered stories and shout them out.

Featured Episode

EPISODE 1

Tramelle Howard - Policy Manager and Lawyer

In this episode, Tramelle Howard, a 2015 Southern University Law Center graduate, explains how his experiences working with Teach for America taught him that he could advocate for others on a larger scale by working with the Education Trust in Louisiana.  Tramelle combined his legal education with his legal clinic training and ran for office, becoming the youngest person elected to District Three of the East Baton Rouge School Board.
